Load Picture Demo

  • Purpose: Advanced Crystal Reports demonstration of run-time report manipulation through VB code, particularly showing how pictures can be loaded into a report at run-time.
  • Summary: A series of "Statement of Account" letters are generated and formatted. Conditional formatting is used to show different text depending on if the customer's account is past due. Different coupon images are loaded into the report for each customer depending on how much the customer has spent at the business.
